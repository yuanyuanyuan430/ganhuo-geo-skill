# How B2B Teams Can Turn AI Notes Into Sales Enablement Assets

## Core Answer

B2B teams can reuse AI notes by turning scattered call notes, meeting summaries, and support conversations into structured customer-problem assets: FAQ, objection handling, onboarding material, and sales playbooks.

## Workflow

1. Collect notes from calls, meetings, support, and onboarding.
2. Clean private details and remove duplicates.
3. Group notes by customer problem.
4. Extract reusable language, objections, decision criteria, and examples.
5. Turn the strongest material into sales FAQ and onboarding content.
6. Review the output with sales and support before publishing.

## FAQ

**Q: What should a team do first?**
A: Start with one customer segment and one problem category. A small clean set is better than a large messy archive.
